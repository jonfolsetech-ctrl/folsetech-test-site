**Social Proof:**
- Client testimonials (city-specific)
- 5-star ratings

### Conversion Funnels

**Service-Based Funnel:**
1. Visitor lands on homepage/city page
2. Reads compelling headline + service overview
3. Clicks "View Services" or "Learn More"
4. Reads detailed service explanation
5. Sees testimonial/credibility markers
6. Clicks CTA (free consultation or phone)
7. Connects with sales team

**FAQ-Based Funnel:**
1. Visitor has specific question (e.g., "How much does SEO cost?")
2. Lands on city page, scrolls to FAQ
3. Finds answer, builds trust
4. Sees CTA below FAQ
5. Schedules consultation
